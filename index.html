Our Mission

Over 750 million adults worldwide are considered illiterate. They can speak and understand their native language but cannot read or write. Millions of people in rural and underdeveloped areas lack access to simple, affordable, and reliable travel booking systems. Most platforms assume digital literacy, banking access, or English fluency. Many struggle with hidden charges, confusing apps, or cannot confidently plan their trips. V Trip is more than just a travel app. It is a bridge to digital mobility for every human, regardless of literacy, location, or language. We believe travel is a right, not a privilege, and we are building the first AI-powered platform that speaks with you, helping underserved communities and first-time digital users travel smarter and safer.

Reducing Digital Travel Inequality
